Dragon Project

The Dragon Project is a mock client website setup. Acting as if SpaceX had hired me to create a website for their Dragon spacecraft. Using all of the knowledge from the year, we create our micro webpage with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancment is a web development style that roots itself from the content and expands from there. Like writing a novel, or any bit of text for that matter, the meaning is the most important part of the piece. The meaning, or content in this case is the most important part, it lays out the information we want on our site, and gives us a frame of reference as we style and build to make our web pages look and feel smooth. click here to see an additional piece on the topic.
